Main Hotel & Suites

Located at J. Panis St., Old Banilad Road, Brgy. Kasambagan, Cebu City, Philippines. Nearest Landmark: at the back of Phoenix Gasoline Station.
OVERVIEW
Location.

Situated in the heart of Cebu, Main Hotel & Suites is close to IT Park, Gaisano Country Mall, Crossroads, Cebu Country Club .and Ginza Restaurant. Also nearby are Ayala Business Park and SM Mall.

 Hotel Features.

Dining options at this business-friendly hotel include a cafe. Public areas are equipped with wireless Internet access free of charge. Guest parking is complimentary. This is a smoke-free property.

Guest rooms.

All air-conditioned guestrooms at Main Hotel & Suites include wireless Internet access free of charge. Televisions are equipped with cable with International Channels. All accommodations provide free local calls (restrictions may apply). Bathrooms offer complementary toiletries. Amenities available on request include in-room massages, by appointment to be booked in advance prior to check in.Shuttle to and from airport is also available upon request with advance booking prior to arrival.
